the history of manhwa, manhua and manga all start in by the word Manga


The "Manga" word derived from a scroll dating back to the 12th century,
are believed to lay the foundation for a right-to-left reading style.
The term was first, widely used in the late 18th century.
Manga is a Japanese term that can be translated into "comic".


Historians and writers on the history of comics explained the two broad
and complementary processes that formed modern comics.
